About : Artistic Touch

Artistic Touch, as the name suggests, is a site that has some beautiful images of art clicked by Shreya Langar.It also shows the art work done by her.

Art is the best way to express yourself, to show to other people how you see the world.

Imagination plays a key role in making any piece of art. True art is something that should be able to connect the spectator with the overall vision of the artist.Art, in any form, can give people emotions that can lift up their spirit and make them more driven than ever.
